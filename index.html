FAQ

Quick answers about AuraCache.

What can I save with AuraCache?

You can save social posts, shopping products, study and homework resources, articles, videos, movie and series pages, recipes, travel ideas, and other useful links.

Is AuraCache a social network or streaming service?

No. AuraCache does not host social posts, movies, or series. It helps you save and organize links to content found on other websites and apps.

Does AuraCache require AI?

No. Core saving, organization, shopping, and reminder features work without AI. Optional supported API-key features can improve titles, descriptions, and summaries.

How is AuraCache different from bookmarks?

Bookmarks mainly store pages. AuraCache adds focused organization, shopping areas, and reminders so saved links are easier to understand and revisit.
