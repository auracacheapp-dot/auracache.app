FAQ

Quick answers about AuraCache.

What can I save with AuraCache?

Social posts, shopping products, study resources, articles, videos, movie and series pages, recipes, travel ideas, and other useful links.

Is AuraCache a social network or streaming service?

No. AuraCache helps you save links to external content. It does not host social posts, movies, or series.

Does AuraCache require AI?

No. Core saving, organization, shopping, and reminders work without AI. Optional supported API-key features can improve titles and summaries.

How is it different from bookmarks?

AuraCache adds intent, reminders, shopping organization, and a focused app experience around saved links.
