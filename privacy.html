Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how AuraCache handles information related to saved links, folders, reminders, shopping links, previews, search, profile settings, and account controls.

Last updated: July 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

AuraCache processes information you choose to add to the app, including saved URLs, titles, descriptions, thumbnails or other link metadata, folders, categories, reminder details, language preferences, profile settings, and account settings.

We may also process basic technical information needed to keep the app reliable, such as app version, device type, diagnostic events, and crash information.

2. Saved Links and Metadata

When you save a link, AuraCache may store the URL and related metadata so the item is easier to recognize, search, organize, and revisit later. AuraCache does not own or control the third-party websites or apps behind saved links.

3. Reminders and Notifications

If you create reminders, AuraCache uses reminder information to notify you about saved links at the time you choose. You can manage notification permissions in your Android device settings.

4. Account, Profile, and Settings

AuraCache may use account and profile information to provide app access, profile settings, language preferences, privacy controls, and account management features.

5. Languages

AuraCache supports English, Turkish, and Spanish. Your language setting is used to display the app experience in your selected language where available.

6. How We Use Information

We use information to provide link saving, folders and categories, reminders, shopping link organization, previews, metadata, search, profile settings, privacy controls, account deletion, support, security, and app reliability.

7. Third-Party Links

AuraCache helps you save and open links from third-party websites, apps, stores, and services. Those third parties have their own terms and privacy policies when you visit or use them.

8. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect app data. No app, device, or network can guarantee absolute security, so avoid saving highly sensitive information in links or notes unless you are comfortable managing it in the app.

9. Your Choices

You can manage saved links, folders, reminders, notification permissions, profile settings, language preferences, and privacy options from the app or your device settings.

10. Delete Account

You can request account deletion from the app where available or by following the instructions on the Delete Account page. You can also contact hello@auracache.app.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect product, legal, or operational changes. The latest version will be posted on this page.

12. Contact

For privacy questions, contact hello@auracache.app.
