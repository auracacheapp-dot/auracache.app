Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how AuraCache plans to handle information related to saved links, reminders, app activity, and future Pro features.

Last updated: May 2026

AuraCache is preparing for public launch. This Privacy Policy may be updated before the app becomes publicly available on app stores. The final policy will reflect the exact features, services, analytics tools, billing systems, and data handling methods used in the released version.

1. Information We May Collect

AuraCache is designed as an action-based memory system for saved links. Depending on the features you use, the app may process information such as saved URLs, link titles, descriptions, categories, reminders, user preferences, app settings, and basic diagnostic information.

If account-based features are added, AuraCache may also process account-related information such as an email address, authentication identifier, subscription status, and basic profile settings.

2. Saved Links and User Content

When you save a link in AuraCache, the app may store information related to that link, including the URL, detected title, description, thumbnail, category, reminder status, and action suggestions. This information is used to help you organize, revisit, and act on saved content.

AuraCache does not own the content behind third-party links. The websites, apps, platforms, marketplaces, and services connected to saved links remain controlled by their respective owners.

3. Local Storage and App Data

AuraCache is planned with a strong local-first product direction. This means many saved items, preferences, reminders, and organizational data may be stored on your device. Local storage helps keep the app fast and gives users more direct control over their saved content.

If optional cloud sync, account backup, shared folders, or cross-device features are added in the future, this policy will be updated to explain what data is synced, where it is processed, and how users can manage it.

4. Notifications and Reminders

AuraCache may use notification permissions to send reminders about saved links, product links, articles, videos, reports, or other content you choose to save. These reminders are intended to help you return to useful content at the right time.

You can control notification permissions through your device settings. If notifications are disabled, reminder-related features may not work as expected.

5. Analytics and Diagnostics

AuraCache may use analytics and diagnostic tools to understand app performance, crashes, feature usage, and general product quality. These tools help improve reliability, fix bugs, and make the app easier to use.

Analytics data, if used, will be configured to support product improvement and app stability rather than selling personal user data.

6. Pro Features and Billing

AuraCache may offer Pro features after launch. Pro may include advanced smart reminders, AI-powered insights, similar content discovery, advanced smart categories, shopping-related features, and an ad-free experience.

Payments for Pro features are planned to be handled through official app store billing systems, such as Google Play Billing. AuraCache does not plan to directly collect or store full payment card details inside the app.

7. Ads and Rewarded Credits

The free version of AuraCache may include ads or rewarded credit experiences. Rewarded credits may allow users to unlock limited AI or premium-style actions by watching an ad or completing an eligible action.

If ads are used, third-party ad networks may process limited device, advertising, and usage-related information according to their own privacy policies. The final public version of this policy will identify any active ad or analytics providers used by AuraCache.

8. AI Features

AuraCache may include AI-assisted features such as summaries, action suggestions, content understanding, and similar content discovery. These features may process saved link metadata, user-selected content, or related context in order to generate useful results.

AI features may be limited, optional, credit-based, or Pro-only depending on the final product design.

9. Data Security

AuraCache aims to use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect user data. However, no app, device, network, or online service can guarantee absolute security.

Users should avoid saving highly sensitive information inside links or notes unless they are comfortable managing that information inside the app.

10. Third-Party Links and Services

AuraCache helps users save and organize links from third-party websites, apps, social platforms, marketplaces, and content providers. When you open or interact with those third-party services, their own terms and privacy policies apply.

11. Children’s Privacy

AuraCache is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law to use digital services without parental consent. If the public launch version includes age-specific requirements, this section will be updated accordingly.

12. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as AuraCache evolves. Changes may be made to reflect new features, app store requirements, legal requirements, analytics tools, billing systems, or data handling practices.

13. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact: hello@auracache.app

This document is a pre-launch policy draft and should be reviewed before public app release.
