Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how AuraCache collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the AuraCache mobile application and website.

Last updated: July 2026

AuraCache is a link organizer app that helps users save useful links, organize them with folders and categories, create reminders, manage shopping links, and revisit saved content later.

1. Information We Collect

AuraCache collects and processes information needed to provide app functionality, improve reliability, support account features, and maintain app quality.

Depending on how you use the app, this may include saved URLs, link titles, descriptions, thumbnails, folders, categories, notes, reminder dates, notification preferences, app settings, language preferences, and basic usage information.

2. Account Information

AuraCache uses Firebase Authentication to support account login and user identification. When you create or use an account, we may process information such as your email address, authentication identifier, provider information, and basic account status.

This information is used to keep your account secure, associate saved data with your account, and provide account-related features.

3. Saved Links and User Content

When you save a link, AuraCache may store information related to that link, including the URL, detected title, description, preview image, category, folder, note, reminder status, and timestamps.

AuraCache does not own or control the external content behind saved links. Third-party websites, platforms, marketplaces, and services remain responsible for their own content, privacy practices, and terms.

4. Local Storage and Cloud Storage

AuraCache stores app data locally on your device to make the app fast and usable. Some account-related data and saved content may also be stored using Firebase services, such as Cloud Firestore, when cloud-backed features are used.

Local device data may be removed if you uninstall the app, clear app data, or delete content inside the app. Cloud-associated data may remain linked to your account until it is deleted through supported app flows or by requesting account deletion.

5. Reminders and Notifications

AuraCache uses notification permissions to send reminders for saved links, shopping links, articles, videos, ideas, or other content you choose to revisit later.

You can manage notification permissions through your device settings. If notifications are disabled, reminder features may not work as expected.

6. Analytics

AuraCache uses Firebase Analytics and Google Analytics to understand general app usage, feature performance, and product quality.

Analytics helps us understand which features are used, detect usability issues, improve app design, and make AuraCache more reliable. We do not use analytics to sell personal user data.

7. Crash Reports and Diagnostics

AuraCache uses Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports, error logs, device information, operating system information, and diagnostic data related to app stability.

This information helps us identify bugs, fix crashes, improve performance, and maintain a stable app experience across different Android devices.

8. Advertising

AuraCache may display ads through Google AdMob and related Google advertising services. Advertising services may process device identifiers, advertising identifiers, approximate usage information, and ad interaction data.

Ads help support the free version of AuraCache. Google and its advertising partners may process ad-related data according to their own privacy policies and applicable consent requirements.

9. AI and Smart Features

AuraCache includes optional smart features that may help improve link titles, metadata, summaries, categories, and content organization.

These features may process saved link metadata, user-selected text, URLs, titles, descriptions, and related content needed to generate useful results. AI-assisted features are optional and may be limited, credit-based, or subject to availability.

10. Purchases and Pro Features

AuraCache may offer premium or Pro features. If purchases are available, payments are handled through Google Play Billing.

AuraCache does not directly collect or store full payment card details. Purchase processing, billing records, refunds, and payment security are handled by Google Play according to Google Play policies.

11. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data. These measures may include secure authentication, provider-managed infrastructure, limited access, and platform security controls.

No app, device, network, or online service can guarantee absolute security. Users should avoid saving highly sensitive information in links, titles, notes, or reminders unless they are comfortable storing that information inside the app.

12. Data Sharing

AuraCache does not sell personal user data.

We may share or process data with service providers that help operate the app, including Firebase, Google Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, Google AdMob, Google Play services, hosting providers, and other infrastructure providers required to deliver app functionality.

We may also disclose information if required by law, legal process, security needs, fraud prevention, or to protect the rights and safety of users, AuraCache, or third parties.

13. Third-Party Links

AuraCache helps users save and organize links from third-party websites, apps, social platforms, marketplaces, and content providers.

When you open or interact with those third-party services, their own terms and privacy policies apply. AuraCache is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party services.

14. Children’s Privacy

AuraCache is not designed for children under the age required by applicable law to use digital services without parental consent.

If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child in a way that does not comply with applicable law, we will take appropriate steps to delete that information.

15. User Choices

You can control several types of data and permissions through the app or your device settings, including notifications, saved links, folders, reminders, app language, and account access.

You can also manage ad personalization and certain Google-related privacy settings through your Google account and Android device settings.

16. Account and Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your AuraCache account and associated data by visiting: https://auracache.app/delete-account/

You may also contact us by email for account or privacy-related deletion requests.

17. International Processing

AuraCache and its service providers may process information on servers located in different countries. By using the app, you understand that your information may be processed outside your country of residence where privacy laws may differ.

18. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect product changes, legal requirements, app store requirements, service provider changes, or improvements to our data practices.

When we update this policy, we will change the "Last updated" date on this page.

19. Contact

For privacy-related questions, account deletion requests, or data inquiries, contact: hello@auracache.app
