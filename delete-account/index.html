Account Deletion

Delete Account

You can request deletion of your AuraCache account and related app data using the steps below.

Last updated: July 1, 2026

How to Request Deletion

Open AuraCache, go to Profile or Settings, and use the account deletion option if it is available in your app version.

You can also email hello@auracache.app from the email address connected to your AuraCache account. Use the subject line "AuraCache Account Deletion Request".

What We Delete

After verifying the request, we delete or anonymize account information associated with your AuraCache account, including saved links, folders, categories, reminders, shopping links, profile settings, and related account data where applicable.

What May Be Retained

Some limited records may be retained when required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, dispute resolution, or service operations. Retained data is limited to what is necessary for those purposes.

Processing Time

Deletion requests are reviewed as soon as reasonably possible. We may contact you if additional verification is needed to protect the account.

Contact

For account deletion or privacy questions, contact hello@auracache.app.
